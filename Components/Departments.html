Departments

Our Departments

This are our various departments

Cardiology Department

This is where patients go for the diagnosis and treatment of
heart-related conditions, such as heart didease, heart attacks and arrhythmias

Emergency Department

The emergency department is where patients go for immediate medical attention when they are experiencing acute medical conditions, such as heart attacks, strokes, and injuries.

Surgery Department

The surgery department is where patients go for surgical intervention to treat medical conditions or injuries.

Outpatient Department

The outpatient department is where patients go for medical consultations, diagnostic tests, and minor procedures. This department is designed to provide medical care to patients who do not require admission to the hospital.

Inpatient Department

The inpatient department is part of the hospital where patients are admitted for medical treatment and care. This department is designed to provide medical care to patients who require extended hospitalization.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

The intensive care unit (ICU) is a specialized department in the hospital that provides critical care to patients who are in life-threatening conditions or require constant monitoring and treatment.

Neurology Department

The neurology department is where patients go for the diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions, such as stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinsonâ€™s disease.

Psychiatry Department

The psychiatry department is where patients go for the diagnosis and treatment of mental health conditions, such as depression, anxiety, and bipolar disorder.

Radiology Department

The radiology department is part of the hospital where diagnostic imaging tests are performed. This department is designed to provide medical imaging services to patients.

Laboratory Department

The laboratory department is part of the hospital where diagnostic tests are performed on patient samples, such as blood and urine. This department is designed to provide medical laboratory services to patients.

Pharmacy Department

The pharmacy department is part of the hospital where medications are prepared and dispensed to patients. This department is designed to provide safe and effective medications to patients.

Rehabilitation Department

The rehabilitation department is where patients go for the treatment of physical or cognitive disabilities, such as after a stroke or injury.
